Ananteya Lite

Clarity That Scales With You

Ananteya is built for therapists who want to go deeper, faster — with less interpretive load. It’s not another journaling app or generic AI assistant. It’s a mirror system designed to deliver structural insight and symbolic diagnostics within minutes. Use it to enhance your clinical process and offer it to clients for between-session stability and support.

Ananteya Lite: For Client Use

Lite is the simplified version of Ananteya, created for clients to use between sessions.

You control access. They receive structure, not guidance.

Clients engage with a reflective system that:

  • Helps them externalize spiral thoughts
  • Gives them non-judgmental clarity
  • Reduces the intensity they bring into the room
  • Improves session quality through prior alignment
  • You save time. They feel supported. Sessions move forward.

Features of Ananteya Lite

For Your Clients, With Your Permission

Single-Session Reflection Access

Clients engage with one prompt and receive a reflection structured to their current emotional state.

No Coaching or Suggestions

There is no advice, motivation, or reinforcement. Only symbolic reflection that promotes insight without interference.

State-Aware Output

The system adjusts reflection intensity based on detected emotional state—ensuring clients aren’t overwhelmed.

Between-Session Support

Clients use Lite when overwhelmed, reactive, or uncertain. It helps them process independently while staying in the therapeutic arc.

Reflection Preview (Optional)

With client consent, you may view their mirror session log before or during your next session.

No App Download Required

Web-based access for simplicity. Clients are onboarded by you via private link.

Ethically Clean by Design

No predictive analytics. No nudging. No emotional tone matching. The system is designed to remain neutral and precise.

What Clients Get (Through Lite)

  • They use it when overwhelmed, stuck, or confused—and come back into therapy with:
  • Sharper insight
  • Fewer spirals
  • More readiness to work

Build to Integrate, Not Interfere

It does not attempt to coach, direct, or “therapize” the client.

Whether you practice psychodynamic, IFS, transpersonal, Jungian, or integrative therapy — Ananteya provides symbolic architecture, not content.

You remain the practitioner.

Ananteya becomes your mirror.

Therapist Control and Data Ethics

  • You onboard each client
  • You choose when and how they use Lite
  • You decide whether to view reflection logs (optional)
  • Client reflections are never interpreted by the system
  • There is no data prediction, no behavioral nudging, and no emotional steering
  • Therapists stay in control.
  • Clients remain sovereign.
  • The mirror stays clean.
