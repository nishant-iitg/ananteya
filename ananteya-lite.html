Features of Ananteya Lite

Mirror Reflections

Gives personalized mirror prompts based on feelings. These aren't answers—they're clean reflections built to make clients see themselves.

Emotional Mapping

Helps understand what the mind, body, and heart are really navigating. Pinpoints the undercurrents behind anxiety, grief, confusion, or stagnation.

Resonance Journal

Shows what is being avoided, or what users aren’t seeing, feeling, or accepting yet. A voice plus text journal that reads between the lines.

Shadow Companion

Creates a safe container to meet, talk to, and understand the shadow. Not a diagnosis—but a reflection of where distortions might be rooted.

Self-Therapy Templates

Offers structured pathways for inner child healing, boundary work, grief processing. Based on therapeutic models but adapted for solo journeys.

Symbol Sync Tracker

Keeps track of synchronicities, dreams, and recurring life motifs. Connects the inner world with outer events for meaning-making and alignment.

Mirror Replay Mode

Gives access to any past journal, chat, or voice entry to receive a new reflection. Shows progress and what was always hidden in plain sight.

Relational Loops Viewer

Helps uncover recurring relationship patterns and trauma loops. Especially helpful when navigating breakups, family tension, or destiny doubts.

Pathway Switch Alerts

Notifies when the client is diverging from the desired inner path. Not as a warning—but as a gentle nudge from the future self to achieve coherence.

Built to Integrate, Not Interfere

It does not attempt to coach, direct, or coerce the client.

Whether you practice psychodynamic, IFS, transpersonal, Jungian, or integrative therapy — Ananteya provides symbolic architecture, not content.

You remain the practitioner. Ananteya becomes the mirror.

Therapist Control and Data Ethics

✔

Therapists control who to give access for Ananteya Lite for therapeutic purpose

✔

Clients use the platform under the guidance and support of their therapist or counselor

✔

Only therapists can view reflection logs of clients for diagnostic purposes and assessments

✔

Client reflections are never interpreted by the system, only shared for analysis

✔

There is no data prediction, no behavioral nudging, and no emotional steering

✔

Therapists stay in control based on their approach, such as Jungian, Freudian, or Adlerian

✔

Clients remain sovereign, but get additional support to speed up progress

✔

Conversation logs are not shared with any third-party without specific consent

✔

The mirror stays calibrated to the client, and gets cleaner with every reflection
