Clarity That Scales With You

Ananteya is built for therapists who want to go deeper, faster — with less interpretive load. It’s not another journaling app or generic AI assistant. Features of Ananteya Lite

For Your Clients, With Your Permission

Single-Session Reflection Access

Clients engage with one prompt and receive a reflection structured to their current emotional state.

No Coaching or Suggestions

There is no advice, motivation, or reinforcement. Only symbolic reflection that promotes insight without interference.

State-Aware Output

The system adjusts reflection intensity based on detected emotional state—ensuring clients aren’t overwhelmed.

Between-Session Support

Clients use Lite when overwhelmed, reactive, or uncertain. It helps them process independently while staying in the therapeutic arc.

Reflection Preview (Optional)

With client consent, you may view their mirror session log before or during your next session.

No App Download Required

Web-based access for simplicity. Clients are onboarded by you via private link.

Ethically Clean by Design

No predictive analytics. No nudging. No emotional tone matching. The system is designed to remain neutral and precise.
