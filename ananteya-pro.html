Features of Ananteya Pro

Instant Structural Reflection

Enter a client name or focus state. Receive immediate pattern-based diagnostic insight—without narrative input or time-consuming journaling.

Symbolic Pattern Recognition

Surfaces core themes, emotional entanglements, or archetypal loops—useful for pre-session clarity or post-session integration.

Mirror Calibration

Scan for emotional state shifts in clients (or yourself) across sessions. Track alignment without overanalyzing.

Resonance Mapping

Detect where clients are stuck symbolically—across relationship loops, identity conflicts, or karmic echo threads.

Case Reflection Logs

Keep personal notes tied to reflection sessions (never client data). Use as a reference without compromising privacy.

Non-Invasive Workflow

Use before or after a session. No obligation. No disruption to your practice.

Private Use, Full Control

Built for therapists only. Nothing is shared or monitored. You remain the sole user and interpreter.

Summarised Weekly Reports

Receive a weekly summary of your clients’ progress, including key insights, patterns, and trends.
