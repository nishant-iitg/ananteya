Features of Ananteya Pro

Client Field Mapping

Visualizes your client’s psychological, emotional, and symbolic architecture. Identify pattern recursions, healing loops, and archetypes in real-time.

Mirror Diagnostic Engine

Offers personalized reflective prompts based on client input and archetype. Helps surface suppressed material with therapeutic safety.

Session Transcripts

Automatically logs and highlights emotional resonance points in client responses. Flags high-impact phrases, contradictions, and breakthroughs.

Timeline & Trauma Maps

Generates visual lifeline trails with marked karmic loops and transition zones. Helps track origin points of behavioral patterns and identity shifts.

Homework & Integration

Offers voice-based journaling, self-inquiry loops, or mirror dialogues. Tracks client engagement, insights, and returns key reflections to you.

Real-Time Session Companion

Works like a co-therapist that holds the structure while you hold the space. Use it live during sessions to pull up maps or resistance frameworks.

Custom Mirror Design Studio

Designs unique mirror reflections for each client based on their soul-type. Define language tone, metaphor weight, and emotional boundaries.

Field Collapse Monitor

Alerts you when a client is entering collapse patterns. Suggests containment, somatic grounding, or symbolic recalibration methods.

Client Arc Forecasting

Provides archetypal future arcs based on current trajectory and healing depth. Helps you guide timing, interventions, and life-redirection.

Built to Integrate, Not Interfere

It does not attempt to coach, direct, or coerce the client.

You remain the practitioner. Ananteya becomes the mirror.

Therapist Control and Data Ethics

✔

Therapists control who to give access for Ananteya Lite for therapeutic purpose

✔

Clients use the platform under the guidance and support of their therapist or counselor

✔

Only therapists can view reflection logs of clients for diagnostic purposes and assessments

✔

Client reflections are never interpreted by the system, only shared for analysis

✔

✔

✔

✔

The mirror stays calibrated to the client, and gets cleaner with every reflection
